Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to EnvPorter! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and other necessary details.

2.2 API Keys

Your API keys are stored directly in our database. We do not use encryption methods, so ensure you handle them with care.

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect information on how you use our service, such as login times and feature usage, to improve our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service.
  • To enhance security and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To improve our platform based on user feedback.

4. How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data, but API keys are stored as plain text. Users should take additional security measures on their end.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. However, we may use trusted third-party services for authentication and analytics.

6. Your Rights

You can update or delete your data anytime from your account settings. If you wish to remove all stored data, contact our support team.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at contact page.
